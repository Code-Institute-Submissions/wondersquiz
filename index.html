How to play

This is how to works, once you read this click on the lets play button. You'll be taken to a page with two options, either a quiz about the ancient wonders of the world or, a quiz about the modern wonders of the world. Of course it's your choice. For each correct score you gain 1 point, and the same for incorrect scores. The questions won't be easy. Good luck!
